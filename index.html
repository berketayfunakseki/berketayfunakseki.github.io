"Tourism Dependence and Regional Macroeconomic Resilience in Italy: The Economic Effects of the COVID-19 Shock"
Difference-in-Differences Event Study Panel Data R Eurostat / ISTAT

Panel analysis of 21 Italian NUTS 2 regions over 2015–2024.
